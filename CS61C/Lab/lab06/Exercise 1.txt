Exercise 1 - Inefficiencies Everywhere

1. Max Delay
Max_Delay=CLK_to_Q_Delay+CL_Delay+Setup_Time
         =CLK_to_Q_Delay+(Adder_Delay+Multiplication_Delay)+Setup_Time
         =10ns+(45ns+60ns)+10ns
         =125ns

2. Min Period
Min_Period=Max_Delay=125ns

3. Max Freq
Max_Freq
    =1/Min_Period
    =1/125ns
    =8MHz
